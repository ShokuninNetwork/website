SHOKUNIN MANIFESTO

The foundation of what we believe in and what we’re building

SHOKUNIN is an organisation dedicated to the sustainable creation of tools and environments for creative work, collective organisation, and technical education.

We believe in behaving in a long-term sustainable way, the actions towards others and ourselves included. We are creating tools and environments in a way that we believe is responsible.

We build a core community of a manageable size for productive communication and strong trust, and ensure that members can act in the way they deem most productive without asking for broad permission first.

We believe this is necessary for social scalability, a necessity of our systems to affect significant change in society.

When we approach resource scarcity, SHOKUNIN adopts a role of facilitating communication and collaboration between creators and resource providers.

This is why SHOKUNIN gather.
